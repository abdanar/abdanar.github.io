Let $$x_1, x_2, \ldots , x_n$$ be nonnegative real numbers. Then

$$
\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}x_{i} \geq \left(\prod_{i = 1}^{n}x_{i}\right)^{1/n},
$$

and equality holds if and only if $$x_1 = x_2 = \cdots = x_n$$.
