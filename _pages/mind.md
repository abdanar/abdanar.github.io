## Questions

**1. Can we find an optimal solution to an optimization problem using only a set of prime numbers?**   
**Possible answer:** It may involve integer programming or combinatorial optimization techniques. Due to the limited search space of prime integers, solving the optimization problem is significantly more challenging than common optimization problems.  
**Motivation**: Linear Equations in Primes by Ben Green and Terence Tao.
